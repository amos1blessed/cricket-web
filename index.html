Sound recording and reproduction is the electrical, mechanical, electronic, or digital inscription and re-creation of sound waves, such as spoken voice, singing, instrumental music, or sound effects. The two main classes of sound recording technology are analog recording and digital recording. We are professionals here!

VOICING

Want to record vocals? ARETHA ROOM is our main studio for that, and it’s also perfect for voice-over and audio books. Our engineers speak English and French. What is voicing on an instrument?

In musical arranging and orchestration, voicing is the process of assigning different notes of a chord or melody to different combinations of instruments in the ensemble. This allows each note to have its own timbre and dynamic level, or “weight,” in the overall sound.
